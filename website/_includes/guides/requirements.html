Requirements, Examples and Ideas

So for your Reprint to get approved, you are going to need:

  • A full 3D model of the printer with the mod
  • Updated firmware to integrate with the mod
  • A journal detailing what you did, in a journal.md
  • A README.md including a picture of your CAD rendering

  • Examples

    • Laser Cutter Mod By Rikhav
    • Bed slinger into a belt printer
    • CoreXY to 5 axis printer
    • Extruder to print chocolate
    • A tool changer mod for under $100
    • Printing metal mod

  • Ideas

    • Pellet-based extruder instead of filament
    • Swappable tool heads (laser, pen plotter, CNC)
    • Automatic part removal system
    • Multi-material mixing hotend
    • Vision system for print failure detection
    • Enclosure with active temperature control
